Business Insights

  • Behaviour: donation is the dominant default behaviour, while selling online is lower. This suggests resale loses to convenience, not necessarily lack of interest.
  • Pain points: time, shipping/delivery, and low return on effort are the strongest friction points. Déjà vu should position itself around effort removal and better outcomes.
  • Payment model: commission-only is the strongest selected pricing signal. Per-item membership has a weaker early signal, so it should be tested carefully against commission-first and package options.
  • Seller policy: the model needs pre-screening, clean/condition standards, resale-value criteria, and clear rules for rejected or unsold items to avoid operational cost waste.
  • Buyer-side implication: trust depends on condition, seller/platform credibility, accurate sizing, price, and clear photos. Aesthetic presentation helps, but should support trust rather than replace it.
